Hi! I am Francesco Diana, a third year PhD student at Inria in the [COATI team](https://team.inria.fr/coati/), working under the supervision of Dr. [Chuan Xu](https://sites.google.com/view/chuanxu) and Dr. [Giovanni Neglia](https://www-sop.inria.fr/members/Giovanni.Neglia/).

My research focuses on uncovering privacy vulnerabilities in federated learning by designing novel privacy attacks.
